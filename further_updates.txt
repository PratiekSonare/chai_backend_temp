QUERY TYPES YOU SHOULD EXPLICITLY SUPPORT

A. Schema & Data Discovery Prompts
Examples:
- What fields are available in sales data?
- Which date ranges does this API support?
- What are the allowed enum values for order_status?
- Do we have region-wise data or only country-level?

B. Cross-Field Logical Conditions
Examples:
- Orders where revenue > 10k AND discount < 5%
- Products that sold well in Q1 but poorly in Q2
- Customers who bought A but not B

C. Temporal Reasoning & Time Windows
Examples:
- Compare last 7 days vs previous 7 days
- MoM growth for the past 6 months
- Same period last year
- Rolling 30-day average

D. Aggregation & Grouping (Light Analytics)
Examples:
- Total sales per region
- Average order value by category
- Count of orders per day

E. Cross-API / Cross-Dataset Queries
Examples:
- Sales data joined with inventory
- Orders + customer tier
- Revenue vs marketing spend

F. Derived / Computed Fields
Examples:
- Revenue after tax
- Profit = revenue − cost
- Conversion rate

G. Comparison Beyond Simple Filters
Examples:
- Top 5 vs bottom 5 products
- Region A vs Region B
- This SKU vs category average

H. Data Quality & Completeness Queries
Examples:
- Which records have missing values?
- Are there nulls in price?
- How many orders failed validation?

I. Output-Format-Driven Prompts
Examples:
- Give this as a CSV
- Return a table with only 5 columns
- Paginated response
- JSON grouped by region

J. Constraint-Based Queries
Examples:
- Show me at most 20 records
- Only include high-confidence data
- Exclude test / sandbox records
